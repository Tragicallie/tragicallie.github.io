Foxhounds

Sunny

Sunny is a 14 year old Foxhound who was rescued by a family with 2 small children. It is unknown what kind of life she had before this family rescued her. She was kept in a cage virtually all of the time by this family & was either ignored or picked on by the children.

One of my family members saw what was happening to Sunny & rescued her from the "rescuers" on a trial basis.

As you have probably figured out, Sunny remained with our family!

It is noted below that foxhounds are not good watchdogs. That is not the case with Sunny. She is very protective of her owner & is a great watchdog! (This is probably because she was rescued from a dismal existence with the prior rescuers.) She is a very happy dog who is always smiling.

Foxhounds

Foxhounds are a medium sized dog. These are the different types of Foxhounds:

  • American Foxhound
  • Dumfriesshire Black & Tan Foxhound (now extinct)
  • English Foxhound
  • Welsh Foxhound
  • Penn-Marydel Foxhound

Foxhounds generally have a very docile & sweet disposition. They are easy going & get along well with children & other animals & enjoy human companionship. Foxhounds are very independent & like to follow a scent. As such, they cannot always be trusted off leash. One of the websites I researched said that foxhounds do not make good watchdogs.

Foxhounds can weigh from 65 to 70 lbs for the males & 60 to 65 lbs for the females. They have a short coat that is usually black, orange & white in color. The lifespan of a foxhound is approximately 10 to 13 years.
